My favourite place is Thirupathi

Thirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Its Sri Venkateswara Temple sits atop one of the the 7 peaks of Tirumala Hills, with Golden temple. It's very peaceful to visit the temple for worship and have prasadam.
